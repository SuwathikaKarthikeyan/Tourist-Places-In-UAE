Fujairah Beaches

Al Aqah Beach

Al Aqah Beach, located along the eastern coast of the UAE in Fujairah, is a stunning destination known for its beautiful scenery and crystal-clear waters. It is surrounded by the majestic Hajar Mountains, providing a breathtaking backdrop for visitors. The beach is famous for its calm waters, making it perfect for a variety of water activities such as snorkeling, scuba diving, swimming, and jet skiing. The nearby Snoopy Island offers excellent spots for diving and snorkeling, home to vibrant coral reefs and marine life. Al Aqah Beach is also family-friendly, with its shallow waters making it safe for children. Visitors can relax on the golden sands or explore the surrounding resorts, such as the Le Méridien Al Aqah Beach Resort and Fujairah Rotana Resort and Spa, which provide luxury accommodations and easy access to the beach. The area also offers attractions like the Fujairah Fort,Al Badiyah Mosque,and natural wonders like Wadi Wurayah for hiking and exploring. The best time to visit is between October and April when the weather is more comfortable, making it an ideal destination for both relaxation and adventure.

Khorfakkan Beach

Khorfakkan Beach, located on the eastern coast of the UAE, is a beautiful and tranquil destination in the emirate of Sharjah. This beach is known for its calm, clear waters and is surrounded by stunning cliffs and natural landscapes, making it a perfect spot for relaxation and outdoor activities. The beach offers a variety of water sports, including swimming, kayaking, and paddleboarding, and its gentle waves make it an ideal location for families with children. Khorfakkan Beach is also famous for its picturesque promenade, where visitors can take leisurely walks while enjoying views of the sea and surrounding mountains. The area features several cafes and restaurants, providing a great spot to enjoy local and international cuisine with a view. Khorfakkan’s rich history is also reflected in nearby attractions like the Khorfakkan Amphitheater and the Khorfakkan Corniche, where visitors can learn about the area’s culture and heritage. With its serene atmosphere and beautiful natural surroundings, Khorfakkan Beach is an excellent destination for those looking to unwind or engage in water-based adventures.

Sandy Beach

Sandy Beach, located in the Al Aqah area of Fujairah, is a serene and picturesque spot that offers visitors a peaceful escape along the UAE’s eastern coast. Known for its soft golden sands and clear turquoise waters, this beach is ideal for relaxing and unwinding under the sun. The calm, shallow waters make it a great destination for swimming, while the surrounding mountains add to the natural beauty of the area. Sandy Beach is also popular for water sports such as snorkeling, diving, and kayaking, with the nearby Snoopy Island being a key attraction for those interested in exploring vibrant coral reefs and marine life. The beach is home to several resorts and hotels, making it a convenient choice for visitors looking for beachfront accommodations. With its tranquil environment and stunning views, Sandy Beach offers a perfect blend of relaxation, adventure, and natural beauty, making it one of Fujairah's most beloved coastal destinations.
